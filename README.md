# Title 1 Project
The goal of this project was to combine and analyze several datasets of enrollment and Title 1 allocation information for schools in New Jersey. This was done for an organization dedicated to providing additional STEM learning resources and tutoring for middle school students in New Jersey, so the completion of the project allows this organization to efficiently direct resources towards the schools and districts that would most benefit.

To accomplish this task, the data was organized to identify the districts and schools with the highest rates of middle school enrollment that could be classified as Title 1. Districts were classified first so that the geographical component could be established, answering the question of which areas of the state needed the most support. Then, individual schools in each district were ranked so that the top Title 1 schools in each district could be identified. This then allows the organization to target the cases of schools in better-off areas that have disproportionately high Title 1 enrollments and tailor their outreach programs accordingly.
